Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to the questions we get asked most frequently.

Can I use Infinite Icons in commercial end products?

Absolutely! All icons downloaded under our standard license can be used in personal and commercial web designs, print elements, software, applications, and marketing materials without mandatory attribution.

Do you accept requests for custom icon sets?

Yes, our specialized design team crafts tailored vectors for brands. Select "Custom Icon Design Requests" in the contact form dropdown menu above, and detail your project timeline and preferred guidelines.

What file formats are provided upon download?

We provide clean, fully scalable native SVGs, optimized React components, icon fonts (WOFF2), and raw high-res PNG formats for developers and design system managers alike.
